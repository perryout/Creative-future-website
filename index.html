What we do

Introduction

This programme helps you to develop into a professional of the future. A professional who possesses the qualities to work together in an interdisciplinary way, to be able to look beyond the boundaries of your own field of expertise and from there to establish relationships with your own professional knowledge. You learn to be open to perspectives of others, to be able to think and act in a divergent way, but also to collaborate in projects on solutions for tough and complex issues.

Personal Roadmap

During your personal growth you will learn new skills while following five categories: the Deep Dive, the Adventure, the Skills Mastery, the Community, and Bildung. At Our work you can see some examples of what the students did this year.

Group project

You have to deal with a wicked problem: an issue that invites you to explore from different perspectives where possible solution directions lie. You will provide a useful contribution to the professional field partner (in the form of a prototype) for the solution of his problem.

Who we are

Project based in The Sluisbuurt

CGI is a multidisciplinary program of 30 EC that challenges you to discover new working and thinking patterns and where much of your flexibility and experimentation is required...

During CGI, you will work together with the collaborated client on helping to solve actual issues and problems from the metropolitan context of Amsterdam
